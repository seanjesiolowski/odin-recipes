Avocado-Mango Salsa

Description

Here is colorful salsa featuring avocado and mango.

Ingredients

  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • Â¾ cup chopped red onion
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Kitchenware

  • mixing bowl
  • serving bowl, optional
  • measuring cups + spoons
  • cutting board
  • knife
  • paper towels, always

Steps

  1. Toss together mango, bell pepper, onion, sugar, olive oil, and vinegar in a bowl. Gently fold in diced avocado, and season with salt.
